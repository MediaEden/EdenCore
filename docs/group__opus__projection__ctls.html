Detailed Description

These are convenience macros that are specific to the opus_projection_encoder_ctl() and opus_projection_decoder_ctl() interface. The CTLs from Generic CTLs, Encoder related CTLs, Decoder related CTLs, and Multistream specific encoder and decoder CTLs may be applied to a projection encoder or decoder as well.
